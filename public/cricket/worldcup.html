CRICKET WORLD CUP

Cricket World Cup ,international cricket championship held at four-year interval that is the premier contest in one-day cricket and one of the most-wanted sporting events in the world.

In 1975 the first Cricket World Cup was contested in England as a series of one-day matches of 60 overs per side. It was held outside England,in India and Pakistan, the first time in 1987. The 1987 contest also saw the number of overs per side reduced to 50. In 2007 Australia become the first team to win three consecutive World Cup tournaments.
